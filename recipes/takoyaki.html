Takoyaki

Description

This recipe shows how to make takoyaki, a popular Japanese street food made of octopus-filled savory balls. These delicious snacks are cooked in a special molded pan and topped with takoyaki sauce, Japanese mayonnaise, and bonito flakes.

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup all-purpose flour
  2. 1 egg
  3. 3/4 cup dashi stock
  4. 1/4 cup soy sauce
  5. 1/2 pound octopus, diced
  6. 1/4 cup green onion, finely chopped
  7. 1/4 cup tempura scraps (tenkasu)
  8. Pickled ginger (beni shoga)
  9. Takoyaki sauce
  10. Japanese mayonnaise
  11. Aonori (seaweed flakes)
  12. Katsuobushi (dried bonito flakes)

Steps

  • Step 1

    In a large bowl, mix flour, egg, dashi stock, and soy sauce until smooth to make the batter.

  • Step 2

    Heat takoyaki pan over medium-high heat. Grease the molds generously with oil.

  • Step 3

    Pour batter into the molds, filling them about 80% full.

  • Step 4

    Add diced octopus, green onions, tempura scraps, and pickled ginger to each mold.

  • Step 5

    Once the bottom is cooked, use a bamboo skewer to flip each ball 90 degrees. Add more batter if needed.

  • Step 6

    Continue rotating the balls until they are evenly cooked and golden brown on all sides.

  • Step 7

    Transfer to a serving plate and top with takoyaki sauce, mayonnaise, aonori, and katsuobushi.

  • Step 8

    Serve immediately while hot and crispy on the outside and gooey on the inside.
