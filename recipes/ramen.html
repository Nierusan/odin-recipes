Homemade Ramen

Description

This recipe shows how to make a delicious bowl of homemade ramen with rich broth, tender noodles, and classic toppings. Perfect for cold days, this comforting dish combines savory broth with chewy noodles and various toppings for a satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  1. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  2. 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  3. 2 tbsp sesame oil
  4. 1 onion, sliced
  5. 4 cups chicken broth
  6. 1 tbsp soy sauce
  7. 2 packages ramen noodles
  8. 2 soft-boiled eggs
  9. 2 cups baby spinach
  10. 2 green onions, chopped
  11. Nori sheets (dried seaweed)
  12. Bamboo shoots
  13. Sliced chashu pork (optional)

Steps

  • Step 1

    Heat sesame oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add garlic and ginger, saut√© until fragrant.

  • Step 2

    Add sliced onions and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.

  • Step 3

    Pour in chicken broth and soy sauce. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes.

  • Step 4

    Meanwhile, cook ramen noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  • Step 5

    Add spinach to the broth and cook until wilted, about 1 minute.

  • Step 6

    Divide noodles between two large bowls. Pour hot broth over the noodles.

  • Step 7

    Top with soft-boiled egg halves, green onions, nori, bamboo shoots, and chashu pork if using.

  • Step 8

    Serve immediately while hot. Add additional toppings and seasonings to taste.
